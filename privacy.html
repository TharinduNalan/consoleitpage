Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ConsoleIT Development ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your personal information when you use our mobile applications (the "Applications" or the "Services") published on the Google Play Store and Apple App Store.

1. Information We Collect and Process

Our collection practices depend entirely on the type of application you are using:

  • Applications Running Locally (No Authentication): For our applications that run entirely on your device and do not require user accounts or authentication, we do not connect to external servers, and we do not collect, transmit, store, or process any personal data or usage metrics of any kind. All app configurations, histories, and settings remain stored strictly and securely on your own local device.
  • Applications with Authentication: For our applications that support authentication, we only collect the minimum personal information necessary to deliver, manage, and secure your user account (such as sign-in credentials). No data from local-only or offline apps is ever aggregated or uploaded.
  • Support Communications: If you contact us voluntarily (e.g., via the app store support channels or email), we may receive information such as your contact details or device characteristics. This is used solely to resolve your specific support request and is not used for profiling or marketing.

2. Third-Party Services

Our Applications may utilize third-party services to support app distribution, platform-level configurations, or secure monetization. These services may collect certain device or identifier records as governed by their respective privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services: Used on Android devices to support app distribution, runtime libraries, and ecosystem utilities. Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / iOS Services: Used on iOS devices to support licensing, distribution, and native system utilities. Apple Privacy Policy
  • In-App Transactions: If any of our Applications support in-app purchases or upgrades, transactions are processed directly and securely by Google Play or the Apple App Store. We never collect, process, or store your payment card details, billing addresses, or financial credentials.

3. How We Use Your Information

If we receive your information under any support or communication channels, it is utilized strictly to:

  • Maintain, manage, and troubleshoot our Applications.
  • Respond directly to your support cases, comments, or technical inquiries.
  • Analyze and resolve reported software anomalies, crashes, or bugs.

4. Information Disclosure and Transfer

We do not sell, trade, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties. We will only share your information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Compliance with Laws: We may disclose information if required to do so by local or international laws, regulations, or legal processes.
  • Protection of Rights: To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users, developers, or the public.

5. Service Security

The security of your information is important to us. While we strive to use acceptable means to secure any information you send us, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Applications are not specifically targeted to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and become aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please reach out to us through the official support channels of the respective app stores so we can take immediate corrective measures.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. For Applications where we collect data or maintain active backend services and possess your registered contact information, we may notify you directly of significant changes. Otherwise, for local-only Applications that have no backend connection or where we do not collect contact details, we cannot notify you directly; therefore, please refer to this page periodically to review any up-to-date information regarding our privacy practices.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the developer contact or support options highlighted on our application listings in the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
